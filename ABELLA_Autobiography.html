I hop from one hobby after the other, and that includes writing poems, playing ukulele (minus the singing), playing sports and video games, watching movies, listening to music, mountain hiking, reading books, and drinking coffee (lots of coffee).

I took a Computer Engineering course at the University of San Carlos hoping that I could both enjoy the things that I'm passionate about, and ofcourse earning in the process. While that was ongoing, COVID-19 pandemic happened that added two years of my college life. Although it was challenging with all that has occured, it did not stop me from enjoying my youth while I still can. Just as they say, que será, será, whatever will be, will be, and that is how I have always perceived life.
